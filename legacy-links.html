Legacy Links

Legacy Links is an internet marketing company that provides SEO and SEM services for startups and legal firms.

Our client tasked us with rebranding and redesigning the website's landing page and the "About Us" and Blog pages.

Being unfamiliar with the internet marketing industry, we started with a competitive analysis to understand the field's key marketing patterns.

We then examined Legacy Links' current website, highlighting key sections and pinpointing weaknesses compared to competitors:

  • Case studies are not easily accessible
  • Content copy is too generic and lengthy
  • Design lacks hierarchy and uses dull colors that blend with each other.

Upon synthesizing our research, we determined that the following sections would successfully convey Legacy Links' business:

  • Hero banner that clearly defines the mission and includes a captivating CTA
  • Client exhibition / Case studies to showcase success stories
  • Our services with delightfully animated graphics and descriptive copy
  • Who we work with to specify targeted clients
  • Thought leadership in the form of blog posts
  • Free consultation form to attract potential clients

Next, we conducted a brand exploration, iterating on logos and style tiles, and producing a brand style guide.

Upon meeting with the client, he chose to go with the more corporate design, which was slightly expected. He was very fond of the rocket/space-themed design but felt that it was a bit playful for the industry. Though we proceeded with the other design, I enjoyed exploring ways to incorporate illustrations such as planets and UFOs to capture the rocket/space motif.

After receiving feedback and redesigning, a teammate and I coded out a final responsive site using HTML, CSS, and jQuery.
