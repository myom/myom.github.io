About Legacy Links

The Legacy Links team is dedicated to driving our clients to greater business growth since 2008. We love helping businesses succeed and measure our success by the success of our clients.

Matthew Kligerman, Founder and CEO

Matthew Kligerman founded Legacy Links in 2008 and has been managing the company since, guiding the company's SEO, SEM, and Premium Link Building operations. He has been honing his internet marketing skills since 2005 and is active in the Latin American start-up community, advising internet start-ups and their investors on valuable internet marketing strategy. Matthew also has 3+ years experience working in the financial services industry, growing Citigroup's CTS Securities and Fund Services business for the world's largest institutional investors. Matthew graduated with distinction from Duke University with a degree in Mechanical Engineering and Materials Science and a secondary concentration in Markets and Management. He splits his time between Sao Paulo and New York City and enjoys rock climbing on his days off.

Ken Diamond, Head of Legacy Links Brazil

Ken Diamond is the Head of Legacy Links' Brazil operations, focusing on supporting the growing ecommerce and internet industry of Brazil. He has expertise in SEO & SEM and focuses his skills to help Legacy Links' Brazilian clients grow through the power of search. Before joining Legacy Links, Ken worked at a $3+ billion private family investment firm in New York for 3 years where he focused on operations for start-up businesses in the home health and spa industries. Ken has operational experience in sales & marketing for ecommerce, SaaS software, and manufacturing businesses. He graduated cum laude from Pomona College with a degree in International Relations. In his free time, Ken enjoys playing pick up basketball and soccer. Ken splits his time between New York City and Sao Paulo.

Ryan Howard, Head of Agency, US

A Tar Heel native, Ryan Howard brings a decade of experience in Internet marketing to Legacy Links. With the philosophy of holistic online marketing practices, Ryan has built multiple, successful businesses within the home services, medical, and real estate industries. Ryan is an alumnus of George Mason University and Western Carolina University; he holds a degree in Business Administration and Information Technology. With a background in digital marketing for home services, Ryan has developed many of the top home services sites on the Internet. In private consulting, Ryan has helped multiple brands establish their presence online, dramatically increase revenue, and improve customer retention.

Ryan Willemsen, Head of Business Development & SEO Pro

Ryan Willemsen is Legacy Links' Head of Business Development and one of our in-house SEO experts. Before joining Legacy Links, Ryan spent five years working in the capital markets with a major investment bank and a commodity-focused hedge fund. He has since migrated to the world of SEO, where he finds ample opportunity to flex his Excel modelling muscles while spending time developing customer relationships and playing an integral role in clients' marketing campaigns. Ryan enjoys breaking down highly technical SEO analyses into everyday language and helping clients develop campaigns to maximize their traffic - and their return on investment. Ryan holds degrees with honors in both Economics and Psychology from the State University of New York. In his spare time, Ryan rock climbs extensively and attempts to surf. He lives in San Diego.

Michael Orrison joined Legacy Links in 2009 to manage the company's Premium Link Building Services. He focuses on developing strategic partnerships with SEO and internet marketing agencies that leverage Legacy Links' Premium Link Building Services for their own client-base. Michael is also an in-house SEO expert and drives the success of Legacy Links' client campaigns. He has a background working with computers in network administration since 2004 and graduated with honors from Lake Forest College with a degree in Biology and a second degree in Communications. In his time outside of Legacy Links, Michael enjoys exploring the outdoors around his city of Las Vegas, Nevada.
